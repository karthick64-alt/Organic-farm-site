Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, practices, and services

Organic vs Natural

What's the difference between organic and natural?

Organic products are certified by the USDA and must meet strict standards including no synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or GMOs. "Natural" is not a regulated term and doesn't guarantee the same standards. Our products are USDA Organic certified.

Are your products really 100% organic?

Yes! All our produce is USDA Organic certified. We undergo regular inspections and maintain detailed records of our farming practices to ensure compliance with organic standards.

Storage & Freshness

How long will my produce stay fresh?

Our produce is harvested at peak ripeness and delivered fresh. When stored properly (refrigerated for most items), vegetables typically last 5-7 days, while fruits vary by type. Check our blog for specific storage tips for each product.

What's the best way to store organic produce?

Most vegetables should be stored in the refrigerator in breathable bags. Leafy greens prefer higher humidity. Some items like tomatoes and potatoes should be stored at room temperature. We include storage tips with every order, and you can find detailed guides on our blog.

Delivery

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver within a 30-mile radius of our farm. This includes most of the surrounding cities and towns. Enter your zip code during checkout to confirm delivery availability, or contact us for more information.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery is free on orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a $7.99 delivery fee. We deliver Tuesday through Saturday, and orders placed before 2 PM are delivered the next business day.

Can I pick up my order from the farm?

Yes! You can select farm pick-up during checkout at no additional charge. Our farm stand is open daily from 9 AM to 5 PM. Just let us know your preferred pickup time when placing your order.

Subscriptions

Can I customize my subscription box?

Yes! While our boxes are curated with seasonal favorites, you can add preferences and dietary restrictions in your account settings. We'll do our best to accommodate your preferences while maintaining the seasonal variety.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Absolutely! You can cancel or pause your subscription at any time through your account or by contacting us. There are no cancellation fees, and you can resume whenever you're ready.

What's included in the subscription boxes?

Weekly boxes include 8-10 items, while monthly boxes include 12-15 items. All boxes feature seasonal produce selected at peak ripeness. Monthly boxes also include recipe cards and storage tips. The contents vary based on what's in season.
